Movie Planner vs Kinopoisk: a film database, not a streamer

18 July 2026

If you remember a title and want a real film card — poster, plot, facts, rating, watchlist — that is Movie Planner. We build a film database product for Russia, not a second video player and not a “companion” to someone else’s streaming app.

Kinopoisk, after the Yandex deal, is primarily a streaming product. The catalog serves that model. Compare the products on that axis — not on “we all get along and complement each other”.

Comparison axes

  • Why you open the app. Need a film card and your library — Movie Planner. Need Yandex’s player — Kinopoisk HD.
  • Title page quality. Movie Planner bets on posters, descriptions, facts, cast, and your library status as the core product.
  • Personal layer. Watchlist, watched, ratings, series progress, group plans, AI screenshot search — on top of the database.
  • Search habit. Goal: when a title pops into your head, open Movie Planner first.

Movie Planner

  • film and series database with search and rich cards;
  • personal library — statuses, ratings, notes;
  • series tracker and new-episode alerts;
  • watch plans and groups;
  • web cabinet, iOS, Android, Chrome extension.

We do not compete for a video subscription. We compete for the habit of opening a film database.
